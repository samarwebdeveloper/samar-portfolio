FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a website?

Typically 4-7 days for a basic business website, 2-3 weeks for more complex projects. I provide a detailed timeline before starting any project.

Can I update the website myself?

Absolutely! I provide easy-to-follow documentation and training so you can make updates independently. I'm also available for ongoing maintenance.

Do you provide hosting?

I don't provide hosting directly, but I recommend and help you set up with reliable hosting providers like Netlify, Vercel, or Namecheap.

What is your pricing structure?

Each project is unique. I offer custom quotes based on your requirements. Contact me with your project details for a free consultation and estimate.
